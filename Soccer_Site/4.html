Prospect Lake's Coaching Competencies

Below are the three competencies that we will be covering in this course. Click on each of the topics below to get an idea of what you will learning during this course.

After you have selected all three topics, you can click the Next button at the top of the screen to move on to the first topic "Coaching Methodology".

1 - Coaching Methodology

What are the best ways to coach? How can maximize player development while creating a positive environment for my players? We will discuss these points and more.

2 - Developmental Priorities

What should my players be learning? What activities are beneficial and why? How do I plan a practice session? We will focus on player development in this topic.

3 - Player Safety

How do I create a safe environment for my players to learn? We will cover some key points to remember during your coaching.
